About Us

Crafting Luxury Fragrances Since 1985

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in the heart of Paris, Luxe Fragrances has been creating exceptional perfumes for over three decades. Our passion for crafting unique scents has made us a leader in luxury fragrance industry.

Each fragrance in our collection tells a story, evokes emotions, and creates lasting memories. We source the finest ingredients from around the world, working with master perfumers to create compositions that are both timeless and contemporary.

Our commitment to quality, artistry, and innovation has earned us the trust of fragrance enthusiasts worldwide. From our boutique beginnings to becoming a globally recognized brand, we continue to pursue perfection in every bottle.

Our Journey

1985

The Beginning

Founded by master perfumer Antoine Dubois in a small Parisian atelier, with a vision to create extraordinary fragrances.

1992

First Breakthrough

Launch of "Mystic Rose" - our signature fragrance that became an instant classic and established our reputation.

2001

Global Expansion

Opened flagship stores in New York, London, and Tokyo, bringing our luxury fragrances to an international audience.

2015

Innovation Award

Received the International Fragrance Excellence Award for our groundbreaking sustainable production methods.

2024

Digital Future

Launched our premium e-commerce platform, making luxury fragrances accessible worldwide with personalized recommendations.
